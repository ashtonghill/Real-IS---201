Over 12 years and 25 individuals in the making Our Story

In 2009 our we had a large family with 8 children. Feeling like something was missing we later adopted a sibling group of 5. Four years following that, we felt to adopt another sibling group of 5. 4 years after, you guessed it... We adopted again bringing the total number of chidlren to 23! Of things to note, we are an ethnically diverse family with 15 latino "chocolate" children, and 8 white "vanilla" children. Making us, the SwirlyCone family.

Our Purpose This Website

With everything going on in the world that seems so negative, we would like to share our story with as many people as possible, by introducing you to each member of the family and their personal resume and experiences. Our hope is to help others see the joy of family, no matter the struggle.
